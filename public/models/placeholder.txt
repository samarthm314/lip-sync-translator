This directory contains 3D avatar models in GLB format.

For the demo, the application will use a fallback avatar.

To use real 3D avatars, download and place them here:
- default-avatar.glb
- default-avatar-thumb.jpg
- professional-avatar.glb
- professional-avatar-thumb.jpg
- casual-avatar.glb
- casual-avatar-thumb.jpg
- cartoon-avatar.glb
- cartoon-avatar-thumb.jpg

Avatar models should include:
- Morph targets for lip-sync (viseme_aa, viseme_ih, etc.)
- Basic animations
- Proper scaling and positioning
